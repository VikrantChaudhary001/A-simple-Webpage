Food Ordering

1. Firstly, Open Our site. 2. Search for the Restaurant you want your food from. 3. Now that the restaurant’s page is open, go to “Order Delivery” from the right side. 4. Thereafter you will get the entire menu with prices for each of the dishes. Add the dish you want and it will be redirected to cart 5. After that select the address at which you want your food to be delivered. 6. Choose Payment Option 7. Finally place Your order

Food Quality

Our staff Wear suitable hygienic clothing, including gloves, hair covering, footwear, where necessary. Prevent contamination of ready-to-eat foods from cutting boards, utensils, clothing, raw meat or eggs. Do not touch ready-to-eat foods with bare hands. Cover hair.

Fastest Free Delivery (t&c)

Your order comes with a 30-minute guarantee from the time an order is placed. If the time taken to deliver the order is more than 30 minutes, comes free if it costs under Rs 300. ... In case it is more than 5 items, it is regarded as a bulk order and the guarantee does not apply.
